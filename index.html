In addition, the organization was also founded for an environmental cause. As climate change is deteriorating, countless species of animal are plummeting in number and some are on the verge of extinction. Thus, we choose the Koalas as our mascot not only because of its adorable appearance but also to raise awareness about its rapidly declining population (from between 46,000 and 82,000 in 2018 to between 32,000 and 58,000 in 2021, changing its status from vulnerable to endangered)

This website serves to somewhat capture the most memorable features and interactions within those 5 months and also to pay tribute to all the "dedication" and "hard work" of the members throughout the period.
